4 steps to becoming a digital nomad

Image credit: Christina @ wocintechchat.com/Unsplash

Want to travel the world and continue working?

Work from anywhere as a digital nomad.
Image credit: Daria Mamont/Unsplash

Working remotely and traveling the world can be a fulfilling way to live. Here are steps to making it happen:

1. Get a remote job using the skills that you have

There are many opportunities for remote work, including digital marketing and social media, teaching online, starting your own business, or working for a company that is fully remote. Think creatively about the skills that you have and apply those to a remote position, whether you're working for yourself or someone else.

2. Test out living and working in a different country

If you currently work remotely for your job, try going on a "working vacation" where you spend some time in a different city or country while continuing to work. Explore the new area, and see how you like living somewhere else, before moving and living fully remotely.

3. Save up and start planning early

Start a budget and have enough savings for where you are planning on living. There are certain countries where it is easy to get a visa for working remotely. You may need to prove a certain income and amount in savings in order to be approved. If you're planning on moving to a country with a longer visa process, then make sure to start as early as possible once you've decided on the country.
