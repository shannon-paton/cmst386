Resources

Image credit: Remy_Loz/Unsplash

The following resources can help you get started on your own travel journey.

For paid work opportunities:

  • Remote.co for finding remote work opportunities
  • Idealist has remote and temporarily remote positions, with a focus on work opportunities for social good
  • On Indeed.com you can filter jobs by remote opportunities

For volunteering and cultural exchange:

  • Workaway "for cultural exchange, working holidays and volunteering in 170 countries"
  • WWOOF for worldwide opportunities on organic farms
  • Greenheart Travel has global volunteer positions for teens and adults

Housing and stays:

  • Airbnb has lots of unique stays around the world
  • Hipcamp is a site that lists camping on farms across the US
