Want to travel more?

On this website I share stories, tips, and resources for traveling on a budget. Traveling can become expensive, stressful, and feel like a hassle – which is the opposite of what a vacation should be! I aim to promote “slow travel” in which you really are able to experience a country and its culture. You may have the opportunity to work remotely as a digital nomad or volunteer within the community you’re visiting. Traveling should be affordable and sustainable, and in the blog I explore ways to make this happen.

Learn more about me, read the blog, or find resources to start your own travel journey!

Want to get in touch? Contact me if you have suggestions for future blog posts or want to work together.
